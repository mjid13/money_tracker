Email Configuration

Enter your email server host (e.g., imap.gmail.com).
Enter your email server port (e.g., 993 for Gmail).
Enter your email address.
Enter your email password or app password. For Gmail, use an App Password instead of your regular password.
Check this if your email server requires SSL.
Enter comma-separated list of bank email addresses (e.g., bankmuscat@bankmuscat.com, alerts@bank.com).
Enter comma-separated list of keywords to look for in email subjects (e.g., transaction, alert, notification).
Important Note

The system will only process emails that match your bank accounts. Make sure you have added your bank accounts before fetching emails.

Emails will be filtered based on:

  1. Sender email matching one of the bank email addresses
  2. Subject containing one of the bank email subjects
  3. Email content containing your bank account number or bank name
